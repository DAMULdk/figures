Human Body: Volume and Surface Area Calculation

This section calculates the volume and surface area of a human body based on height and weight.

Calculations for Human Body

To calculate various properties of a human body:

  • Height (h): The height of the person in centimeters.
  • Weight (w): The weight of the person in kilograms.
  • Volume: The volume of a human body is calculated using the formula: V = Weight (kg) / * 1,01523.
  • Surface Area: The surface area of a body is calculated using the formula: A = sqrt((Weight (kg) * Height (cm)) / 3600).

Please provide values for both height and weight to perform calculations.
