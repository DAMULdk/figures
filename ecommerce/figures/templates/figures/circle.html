Circle: Definition and Properties

A circle is a geometric shape characterized by all points in a plane that are a given distance from a given point, called the center.

Calculations for a Circle

To calculate various properties of a circle:

  • Radius: The radius of a circle is the distance from the center to any point on the circle.
  • Diameter: The diameter of a circle is twice the length of the radius. It is a straight line passing through the center of the circle and connecting two points on the circle.
  • Area: The area of a circle can be calculated using the formula: A = πr2, where r represents the radius of the circle.
  • Circumference: The circumference of a circle is the perimeter of the circle and can be calculated using the formula: C = 2πr, where r represents the radius of the circle.

Fun Fact about Circles

A circle is a symmetric shape with infinite lines of symmetry. It is a fundamental shape in geometry and finds applications in various fields such as mathematics, engineering, and design.
