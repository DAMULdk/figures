Rectangle: Definition and Properties

A rectangle is a geometric shape characterized by four right angles and opposite sides that are equal in length. It includes squares as a special case where all sides are of equal length.

Calculations for a Rectangle

To calculate various properties of a rectangle:

  • Side Lengths: The length and width of a rectangle can be determined by measuring any pair of opposite sides.
  • Area: The area of a rectangle is found by multiplying its length by its width. Mathematically, it can be expressed as: A = l * w, where l represents the length and w represents the width.
  • Perimeter: The perimeter of a rectangle is the sum of the lengths of all four sides. It can be calculated as: P = 2(l + w), where l represents the length and w represents the width.
